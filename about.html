EATKON, provides a fully autonomous system for running and maintaining a restaurant in smart way.

ABOUT

Basic concept of our application is that, we are providing full automation to restaurant system in three way

1) Scanning QR code in Restaurant and order food

Scanning QR code in Restaurant and order food:- In this concept when customer enter into a restaurant then he/she has to scan a QR code that is place on table. After that menu of that restaurant will open on customer’s smart phone and he/she can order food from that portal or application. They can add item if they wishes and at last payment gateway.

2) Scanning QR code from car and order food

In this concept a person can order food from their car by scanning QR code that will be displayed outside of the restaurant. Then menu of that restaurant will open and then same method as 1st method.

3) Pre - Order

In this concept customer on weekend get bored to wait for their chance to dine, so we are introducing a new concept of pre-order in this customer will order their food from our application from their home and this order will directly move to restaurant, after this chef will prepare food. So due to this customer will not have to wait for a long time.
